🌌 Timeline of the Universe

A scroll through cosmic history — no scripts, no stylesheets, just HTML.

☄️ 13.8 Billion Years Ago

The Big Bang occurs. The universe begins from an incredibly hot, dense state.

🌟 13.6 Billion Years Ago

The first stars form, lighting up the early universe and fusing hydrogen into heavier elements.

🌀 13.2 Billion Years Ago

Galaxies begin to form, drawn together by the gravity of dark matter.

🌍 4.5 Billion Years Ago

Our solar system forms. Earth emerges as a molten rock, cooling slowly over millions of years.

🌱 3.5 Billion Years Ago

Life begins in the oceans as simple single-celled organisms.

🦖 230 Million Years Ago

Dinosaurs dominate Earth for more than 160 million years.

🚀 1969 AD

Humans land on the moon. Neil Armstrong and Buzz Aldrin take the first steps on the lunar surface.

“That's one small step for a man, one giant leap for mankind.”
